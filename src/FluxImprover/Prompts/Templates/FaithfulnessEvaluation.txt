## Evaluation Instructions

## Scoring
- Score 1.0: All claims are fully supported by the context
- Score 0.75-0.99: Most claims supported, minor unsupported details
- Score 0.5-0.74: Some claims supported, some unsupported or inaccurate
- Score 0.25-0.49: Few claims supported, significant hallucinations
- Score 0.0-0.24: Answer contradicts context or is entirely unsupported
